The Brown Bear

The brown bear is one of the largest and most powerful land carnivores. Known for its strength, intelligence, and adaptability, it is found across North America, Europe, and Asia.

Learn more about the brown bear on Wikipedia

Top 3 Characteristics

  1. Large body and strong limbs
  2. Omnivorous diet, including fish, berries, and small mammals
  3. Excellent sense of smell and memory

Natural Habitats

  • Forests
  • Mountain regions
  • Tundra and arctic coastlines
